How did the coronavirus spread ?

  • December 8 A patient in the city of Wuhan sought medical help for pneumonia-like symptoms.
  • December 31 Local hospitals in Hubei report the first four cases of a "pneumonia of unkwown etiology.
  • December 31Wuhan Municipal Health Authorities report a string of pneumonia-like cases in China.
  • January 1The seafood market(Huanan Seafood Wholesale Market) that was identified as a supply center of the outbreak is closed.
  • January 3China officialy notifies the WHO of an outbreak.
  • January 13Thailand reports its first case, a sixty-one year old woman who did not visit the seafood market.
  • January 14Wuhan implements screening measures for travelers leaving the city at airports, railway stations, and other passenger terminals.
  • February 1First cases appear in United States and Europe.
  • February 3First cases appear in many more countries;travel bans spread.
